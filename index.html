    About Me

    Hope Kemigisha is a Mother, Minister, Social Entrepreneur, and inspirational speaker from Uganda. With over 15 years of experience, she empowers individuals to overcome obstacles and embrace their purpose.

    At age 20, Hope founded an orphanage, sparking a lifelong commitment to nurturing leaders and promoting gender equality. She is the Founder of GABI, an inclusive fashion label empowering women designers and artisans.

    Hope has spoken at esteemed venues like the U.S. Senate and the U.N. Headquarters, advocating for resilient communities and enduring leadership perspectives.

    Balancing her roles as Founder of GABI, Country Director of Global Peace Women, and leading Petals of Hope International, she frequently travels between the U.S. and Uganda.

    Hope holds a degree from Makerere University and continues to inspire others to embrace their true purpose and authority.
